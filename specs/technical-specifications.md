# Nimbus Delta-P Technical Specifications

**Document Type:** Internal Reference
**Last Updated:** January 18, 2026
**Source:** Bimpair OEM documentation, Quote OFR000388

---

## 1. System Overview

The Delta-P is a portable, rechargeable nitrogen adjustment system for shock absorbers and suspension components. It operates at high pressure (120 bar storage / 50 bar output) and is designed for field use without workshop infrastructure.

## 4. Nitrogen vs. Air (Technical Rationale)

Nitrogen is specified over compressed air for the following reasons:

- **Pressure stability over time** — nitrogen maintains consistent pressure longer
- **Humidity insensitivity** — unaffected by moisture and temperature variations
- **Consistent suspension performance** — more predictable damping behavior
- **Reduced internal oxidation** — extends component life and reduces wear
