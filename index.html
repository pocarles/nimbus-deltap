FAQ

Why is the ∆P more expensive than competitors?

Because it works when it arrives. Competitors ship empty — you pay $699, then spend hours finding a shop to fill it. We ship pre-filled, tested, and ready. The premium buys you convenience and time.

How do I refill the cartridge?

We're building a network of certified refill partners across the US. For $30, any partner shop can refill your cartridge while you wait. Partner map launches before first shipments.

What's the output pressure range?

0–50 bar (0–725 PSI), adjustable via the precision bleed valve. The gauge shows both bar and PSI scales.

How many adjustments per cartridge?

Each cartridge holds ~54 liters of N₂. Depending on shock size and pressure, expect 15-30+ full adjustments per cartridge.

Is the deposit refundable?

Yes, 100% refundable anytime before we ship. Email us and we'll process the refund within 48 hours.

Why nitrogen instead of air?

Nitrogen is dry, stable, and inert. Air contains moisture that causes inconsistent damping and corrosion. Every pro race team uses nitrogen.

What does the warranty cover?

2-year warranty covers manufacturing defects in the head unit, gauge, and hose. Cartridges are covered for 1 year.

Do you ship internationally?

Not yet. Due to Hazmat regulations for pressurized nitrogen, we're US-only initially. International planned for late 2026.
