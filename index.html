Our Story

We needed it. So we made it happen.

At Nimbus Suspensions, we have large nitrogen tanks at our facility. But when we're out testing vehicles or tuning on location, dragging heavy equipment isn't practical.

We wanted something we could throw in a truck and adjust shock pressure in seconds—anywhere, anytime.

So we partnered with specialized engineers in Europe to develop the ∆P. A compact, pre-filled system powerful enough for any suspension work, light enough to take everywhere.

What surprised us? Once we started using it, we found ourselves reaching for it even back at the shop. The convenience is that good. Now we're testing if others feel the same way.

The Science

Why nitrogen matters

Consistent performance

Unlike air, nitrogen doesn't contain moisture or oxygen. This means no oxidation, no foaming, and no pressure fluctuations from temperature changes.

Predictable damping

Nitrogen's molecular structure provides more stable compression characteristics than air, giving you repeatable, predictable suspension behavior.

Extended component life

By eliminating moisture and oxygen, nitrogen prevents internal corrosion and seal degradation, dramatically extending shock service intervals.

FAQ

Does it really ship pre-filled?

Yes. We fill and seal every cartridge at our Florida facility before shipping. Unbox it, thread the cartridge onto the head, and you're ready to tune.

How many fills do I get per cartridge?

It depends on your shock volume and target pressure. A typical Nimbus Erebus shock (charged around 80 PSI) can be adjusted 15–30 times from a single cartridge—for most users, that's years of tuning.

Can I refill the cartridge myself?

Not directly. For safety and certification reasons, cartridges must be refilled using our authorized Refill Station network. We're building a partner network of shops equipped to refill cartridges on-site.
